- **Revenue:** $10K–$100K+/month
- **Pain Point:** Successful offline, non-existent or embarrassing online presence

### ICP #2: The DTC E-commerce Brand
- **Verticals:** Skincare, Fashion, Supplements, CPG, Food & Beverage, Home Goods, Pet Care
- **Revenue:** $50K–$350K/month
- **Pain Point:** Spending on paid ads with a leaky post-click experience, weak email marketing, leaving six figures on the table in LTV
- **Psychographics:** Overwhelmed founder or marketing lead, actively hiring for email/retention roles
