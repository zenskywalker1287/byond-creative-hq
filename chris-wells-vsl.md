# CHRIS WELLS VSL — TELEPROMPTER SCRIPT
### ChrisWellsValencia.com | Jarvis Solutions | $700 Offer
### Runtime: ~2 minutes | 2nd Person | Intimate + Motivating

---

## PRE-PRODUCTION NOTES

**Tone:** Calm. Authoritative. Like a trusted advisor pulling you aside — not a salesperson.
**VO Style:** Deep. Measured. Real. No radio energy. Confident and unhurried.
**Music:** Sparse piano or low ambient at open. Swell slightly at the pivot. Pull back to silence at the close.
**Pacing:** Hold silence longer than feels comfortable. Let the words land.

**PRODUCTION WORKFLOW:**
1. Record VO audio first (this script)
2. Build Remotion composition synced to audio
3. Apple-style notification popups (Phase 1B) — Remotion `useCurrentFrame` timed to VO beats
4. Stat cards, side-by-side comparisons, site scroll — all Remotion sequences
5. Final export: 1080p or 4K, h264, ready for send

## PRODUCTION BRIEF

**The Grid Shot is everything.** Do a live screen recording of "best realtor Valencia CA" search results. Let the viewer watch Chris buried in the scroll. Nothing written hits as hard as watching it happen in real time.

**Side-by-side hold:** Show competitor sites vs. ChrisWellsValencia.com for a full 5 seconds with no voiceover. Let the visual do the work.

**The chriswellsre.com line:** If you screen record that domain resolving to nothing — that's a gut punch on its own. 3 seconds of a blank page. No words needed.

**Pacing:** Script runs ~2 minutes at the right speed. Do NOT rush the close. Silence is the weapon in the last 20 seconds.
